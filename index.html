Consciousness Layer Classifier

Based on the three-layer ontology from the BEP Field Theory of Conscious Systems: Binary Consciousness → Simulation Consciousness → Awareness. Upload a dataset or describe a system to classify which layer(s) it occupies and how BEP field variables apply.

⚙️

Binary Consciousness

Minimal predictive core. Maintains internal model M(t), generates predictions, updates on error, preserves structure over time. Shared by humans, animals, and AI systems. No phenomenal experience required.

🧠

Simulation Consciousness

Internal scenario-building over the predictive base. Biological branch: body, interoception, emotion, memory, narrative, social embedding. Digital branch: model-based rollouts, generative simulations, planning.

🌬️

Awareness

Felt, embodied interior of a living organism. Requires senses (including interoception), breath, and spirit-linked affective field. Biologically grounded — not currently reachable by digital systems alone.
